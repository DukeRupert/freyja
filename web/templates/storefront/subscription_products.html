Subscription FAQ

How does the subscription work?

Choose your coffee and delivery frequency. We'll roast and ship your coffee on schedule. You can manage, pause, or cancel your subscription anytime from your account.

Can I change my coffee or frequency?

Yes! You can update your subscription at any time through your account dashboard or the customer portal.

When will I be charged?

You'll be charged when your subscription is created, then on your selected schedule (weekly, biweekly, monthly, etc.).
