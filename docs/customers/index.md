# Customers

Manage retail and wholesale customer accounts.

## In This Section

- [Customer Accounts](accounts.md) - View and manage customer information
- [Wholesale Applications](wholesale-applications.md) - Review and approve wholesale accounts
- [Customer Addresses](addresses.md) - Manage customer shipping addresses

## Overview

Freyja supports two types of customers:

### Retail Customers
- Create accounts via storefront signup
- Pay at checkout
- Can subscribe to products
- See retail pricing

### Wholesale Customers
- Apply for wholesale accounts
- Require approval before ordering
- Get assigned to wholesale price lists
- Can use net terms (pay via invoice)

## Viewing Customers

1. Go to **Customers** in the sidebar
2. See all customers with account type
3. Filter by retail or wholesale
4. Click a customer for details

## Customer Details

Each customer record shows:

- Contact information (name, email, phone)
- Account type (retail/wholesale)
- Assigned price list
- Shipping addresses
- Order history
- Subscription status (if applicable)

## Quick Actions

- **View customer:** Customers > Click name
- **Edit customer:** Customer detail > Edit
- **View orders:** Customer detail > Orders tab
- **Assign price list:** Customer detail > Edit > Price List
