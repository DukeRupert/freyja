# Recording Payments

Track invoice payments from wholesale customers.

## Payment Methods

Wholesale customers can pay invoices via:

### Stripe (Online)
- Customer clicks payment link in invoice
- Pays by card online
- Payment recorded automatically
- Invoice marked as paid

### Manual Recording
For payments received outside Stripe:
- Check
- Bank transfer
- Cash
- Other methods

## Online Payments

When customers pay via Stripe:

1. Customer receives invoice email
2. Clicks **Pay Invoice** link
3. Enters payment information
4. Payment processed
5. Invoice automatically marked paid
6. You receive notification

No action required from you.

## Recording Manual Payments

When you receive payment by check, transfer, etc.:

1. Go to **Invoices**
2. Find the invoice
3. Click **Record Payment**
4. Enter payment details:
   - Amount received
   - Payment method
   - Reference number (check #, transfer ID)
   - Date received
5. Save

### Partial Payments

If customer pays less than full amount:

1. Record the partial payment
2. Invoice shows remaining balance
3. Record additional payments as received
4. Invoice marked paid when fully settled

## Payment Status

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Unpaid | No payment received |
| Partial | Some payment received |
| Paid | Full amount received |

## Viewing Payment History

Each invoice tracks all payments:

1. Open the invoice
2. View **Payments** section
3. See all payments with dates and amounts

## Reconciliation

### Regular Review
- Check invoices match payments received
- Verify bank deposits against recorded payments
- Investigate discrepancies promptly

### Month-End
- Review all outstanding invoices
- Follow up on overdue accounts
- Reconcile with accounting records

## Handling Issues

### Payment Not Received
1. Verify payment method customer used
2. Check bank/Stripe for incoming payment
3. Contact customer to confirm
4. Record once verified

### Overpayment
1. Record actual amount received
2. Contact customer about overage
3. Apply to future invoice or refund

### Wrong Invoice Paid
1. Record payment on correct invoice
2. Update records accordingly
3. Communicate with customer

## Best Practices

### Prompt Recording
- Record payments same day received
- Don't let checks pile up
- Keep records current

### Clear Communication
- Send payment confirmations
- Provide receipts when requested
- Be clear about what's paid

### Documentation
- Note reference numbers
- Record payment method
- Keep supporting documents
