# Storefront

Your customer-facing store and shopping experience.

## In This Section

- [Storefront Overview](overview.md) - Understanding your customer-facing store
- [Cart & Checkout](checkout.md) - The purchase flow
- [Customer Portal](customer-portal.md) - Customer self-service features

## Overview

The storefront is what your customers see when they visit your store:

- Product browsing and search
- Product detail pages
- Shopping cart
- Checkout flow
- Customer accounts
- Order history

## Customer Experience

### Browsing
- View all products
- Filter by attributes
- See pricing and availability

### Product Pages
- Full product details
- Coffee attributes (origin, roast, tasting notes)
- Size and grind selection
- Add to cart

### Cart
- Review selected items
- Update quantities
- Proceed to checkout

### Checkout
- Enter shipping info
- Select shipping method
- Enter payment details
- Place order

### Account
- View order history
- Manage addresses
- Manage subscriptions
- Update payment methods

## What You Control

Through the admin dashboard, you control:

- Products and pricing visible to customers
- Shipping options and rates
- Tax calculation
- Available payment methods
- Subscription options
