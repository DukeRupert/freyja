## Overview

Setting up Freyja involves a few key steps:

1. **Connect Stripe** - Freyja uses Stripe for all payment processing
2. **Configure settings** - Tax rates, shipping options, email
3. **Add products** - Create your coffee catalog with variants and pricing
4. **Set up price lists** - Configure retail and wholesale pricing tiers
5. **Test checkout** - Place a test order to verify everything works
6. **Go live** - Launch your store

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, you'll need:

- A Stripe account (create one at [stripe.com](https://stripe.com))
- Product photos (recommended: square images, at least 800x800px)
- Your coffee product details (origins, roast levels, tasting notes)
- Shipping rates or an EasyPost account for real-time rates

## Time to Launch

Most roasters complete initial setup in 1-2 hours:

- Stripe connection: 10 minutes
- Basic settings: 15 minutes
- First few products: 30-60 minutes
- Test order: 10 minutes
