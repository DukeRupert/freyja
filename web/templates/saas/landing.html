Frequently asked questions

Have a different question? Contact us and we'll get back to you.

Who is Freyja for?

Independent coffee roasters with 1-5 locations doing $200k-2M in annual revenue. If you sell roasted coffee online and want to grow or formalize wholesale, Freyja is built for you.

What payment processor do you use?

Stripe. You'll connect your own Stripe account and pay their standard rates (2.9% + $0.30 per transaction). We don't add any transaction fees on top.

Can I import my existing products?

Yes. We support CSV import for products, customers, and historical orders. We can also help with migration from Shopify, WooCommerce, or other platforms.

How do subscriptions work?

Customers can subscribe to any product with flexible frequencies (weekly to every 2 months). They manage their own subscriptions through a self-service portal, including pause, skip, and cancel.

What about wholesale invoicing?

Wholesale customers can be set up with Net 15 or Net 30 terms. Invoices are generated automatically and sent via Stripe Invoicing. Customers can pay online or by check.

Do you handle shipping?

We integrate with standard shipping carriers for label generation and tracking. You set your own shipping rates or use calculated rates based on weight and destination.

Is there a contract?

No long-term contract. Pay monthly and cancel anytime. Annual plans get a discount but you can still cancel with a prorated refund.

What kind of support do you offer?

Email support with responses typically within one business day. We also have documentation and guides for common tasks.

Can I customize the storefront design?

Yes. You can customize colors, add your logo, and adjust the layout. The storefront is designed to look good out of the box while allowing your brand to shine through.
