Pricing FAQ

Are there any transaction fees?

No. You pay Stripe's standard processing fees (2.9% + $0.30 per transaction), but we don't add anything on top. Your $149/month covers the platform, period.

What's the launch special?

Early customers get their first 3 months at $5/month (normally $149/month). After 3 months, it's $149/month. You can cancel anytime.

Is there a contract?

No long-term contract. Pay monthly and cancel anytime. Your data is yours and we'll help you export it if you decide to leave.

What happens if I outgrow the plan?

The plan has no limits on products, orders, or customers. If you need enterprise features (custom integrations, dedicated support), contact us to discuss.

Can I get a refund?

If you're not happy within the first 30 days, we'll refund your payment. After that, you can cancel anytime but we don't offer prorated refunds.

Do you offer annual billing?

Not yet, but it's coming soon. Annual billing will include a discount. Sign up for monthly now and we'll migrate you when it's available.
