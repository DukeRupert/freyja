About Freyja

Freyja is an e-commerce platform built exclusively for coffee roasters. We provide the business-critical features that small roasting operations need to sell both direct-to-consumer and wholesale, without the enterprise pricing or complexity of general-purpose platforms.

The Problem We Solve

Small coffee roasters face a difficult choice when setting up e-commerce. General platforms like Shopify handle B2C well, but B2B is an afterthought requiring expensive plugins. Wholesale-focused platforms are overbuilt and overpriced for small producers.

The result: roasters either overpay for features they need, cobble together fragile integrations, or simply don't offer capabilities that would grow their business.

Our Approach

By building exclusively for coffee products, we eliminate the complexity that general-purpose platforms must carry. This focus lets us provide integrated B2C and B2B functionality, subscriptions, and invoicing all in one system at a price that makes sense for small businesses.

Why "Freyja"?

Freyja is the Norse goddess associated with prosperity and abundance. The name reflects our goal: helping small coffee businesses thrive.
