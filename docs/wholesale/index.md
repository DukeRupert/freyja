## Overview

Freyja provides full B2B functionality alongside retail sales:

- **Wholesale accounts** - Separate from retail with approval workflow
- **Wholesale pricing** - Different prices via price lists
- **Net terms** - Pay later via invoice
- **Invoicing** - Create, send, and track invoices
- **Consolidated billing** - Multiple orders on one invoice

## Why Wholesale Matters

For coffee roasters, wholesale typically represents:

- Larger order volumes
- Recurring business relationships
- Different pricing and payment expectations
- Café and restaurant accounts

## Wholesale vs Retail

| Aspect | Retail | Wholesale |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| Account | Self-signup | Approval required |
| Pricing | Retail price list | Wholesale price list |
| Payment | At checkout | Net terms or checkout |
| Invoicing | Order receipt | Full invoices |
| Volume | Individual bags | Cases/bulk |

## Workflow Overview

```
Customer applies for wholesale
            ↓
You review and approve
            ↓
Assign price list and terms
            ↓
Customer places orders
            ↓
Generate invoice (or auto-consolidate)
            ↓
Customer pays invoice
            ↓
Record payment
```
