# Wholesale Applications

Review and approve wholesale account requests.

## How Applications Work

1. Customer creates a retail account
2. Customer submits wholesale application
3. You review the application
4. Approve or reject the request
5. If approved, customer gets wholesale access

## Viewing Applications

1. Go to **Customers**
2. Filter by **Pending Wholesale**
3. See all applications awaiting review

Or:

1. Go to customer detail
2. View **Wholesale Application** section

## Application Information

Applications typically include:

- Business name
- Business type (caf√©, restaurant, etc.)
- Tax ID / resale certificate
- Estimated monthly volume
- Business address
- Additional notes

## Reviewing Applications

Consider when evaluating:

- **Legitimate business?** Verify business exists
- **Volume potential** Worth the wholesale discount?
- **Fit** Right type of customer for your business?
- **Red flags** Anything concerning?

## Approving Applications

1. Open the customer detail
2. Review application information
3. Click **Approve**
4. Assign a price list (wholesale tier)
5. Set net terms if offering
6. Save changes

After approval:
- Customer receives notification
- Customer sees wholesale pricing
- Customer can place wholesale orders

## Rejecting Applications

1. Open the customer detail
2. Review application
3. Click **Reject**
4. Optionally add a reason
5. Save changes

After rejection:
- Customer receives notification
- Customer remains a retail account
- Customer can reapply later

## Setting Up Approved Accounts

When approving, configure:

### Price List
Assign the appropriate wholesale tier:
- Start new accounts at entry tier
- Upgrade based on volume over time

### Net Terms
If offering payment terms:
- Net 15 (due in 15 days)
- Net 30 (due in 30 days)
- Net 60 (due in 60 days)
- Or require payment at checkout

### Minimum Orders
Consider setting minimums:
- Minimum order value
- Minimum quantity per order

## Best Practices

### Response Time
- Review applications within 1-2 business days
- Quick responses build good relationships

### Verification
- Google the business name
- Check for social media presence
- Verify tax ID if required in your state

### Communication
- Be clear about expectations
- Explain your wholesale program
- Welcome new wholesale accounts

### Documentation
Keep notes on:
- Why you approved/rejected
- Any special arrangements
- Communication history
