# Product Images

Upload and manage product photos for your catalog.

## Adding Images

1. Open a product for editing
2. Scroll to the **Images** section
3. Click **Upload Image** or drag and drop files
4. Images upload automatically
5. Click **Save Product** to confirm changes

## Image Guidelines

### Recommended Specifications

| Attribute | Recommendation |
|-----------|----------------|
| Format | JPG or PNG |
| Dimensions | Minimum 800x800 pixels |
| Aspect Ratio | Square (1:1) preferred |
| File Size | Under 5MB per image |
| Background | Clean, simple background |

### Photography Tips

- **Lighting** - Natural light or soft studio lighting
- **Focus** - Sharp focus on the product
- **Angles** - Show the bag clearly, consider multiple angles
- **Consistency** - Similar style across your catalog

## Image Order

- The first image is the **primary image** shown in listings
- Drag images to reorder
- Additional images appear in product detail pages

## Managing Images

### Reordering
- Drag and drop to change order
- First position = primary image

### Removing
- Click the delete/remove button on an image
- Confirm removal
- Save the product

### Replacing
- Delete the existing image
- Upload the new version
- Reorder if needed

## Multiple Images

Consider showing:
- Front of bag (primary)
- Back of bag (ingredients, info)
- Beans close-up
- Lifestyle/brewing shot

## Image Storage

Images are stored securely and served from:
- Local storage (development)
- Cloud storage (production)

Large images are automatically optimized for web delivery.
