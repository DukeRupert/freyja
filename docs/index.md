## About Freyja

Freyja is designed for small coffee roasters (1-5 locations) who need:

- **Unified retail and wholesale** - One system for all your sales channels
- **Coffee-specific features** - Origin, roast level, tasting notes built-in
- **Subscription support** - Recurring orders with flexible frequencies
- **Wholesale invoicing** - Net terms and consolidated billing
- **Simple pricing** - $149/month flat fee, no transaction fees beyond Stripe

Built by coffee people, for coffee people.
